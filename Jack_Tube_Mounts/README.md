# Jack Tube Mounts
## Overview
Designed new mounts for the car's jack tube. Previous mounts were no longer legal with the FSAE rulebook as the lower 180 degrees of the jack tube was not exposed. New designed saved 0.2 lbs from the drivetrain system and acheived a higher minimum factor of safety of 3.0.
